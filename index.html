Monkey D. Luffy

The captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy aims to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure, One Piece.

Roronoa Zoro

Zoro is a swordsman and Luffy's first crew member. He dreams of becoming the world's greatest swordsman.

Nami

Nami is the Straw Hat Pirates' navigator and a skilled cartographer.

Usopp

Usopp is the Straw Hat Pirates' sharpshooter and a talented inventor.

Sanji

Sanji is the Straw Hat Pirates' cook and a master of martial arts.

Tony Tony Chopper

Chopper is the Straw Hat Pirates' doctor and a reindeer-human hybrid.

Nico Robin

Robin is the Straw Hat Pirates' archaeologist and a skilled assassin.

Franky

Franky is the Straw Hat Pirates' shipwright and a cyborg.

Brook

Brook is the Straw Hat Pirates' musician and a living skeleton.

Jinbe

Jinbe is a fish-man karate expert and a former member of the Seven Warlords of the Sea.
